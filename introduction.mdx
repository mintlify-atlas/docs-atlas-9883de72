# Welcome to LobeHub

LobeHub is the ultimate space for work and life: to find, build, and collaborate with agent teammates that grow with you. We're building the world's largest human-agent co-evolving network.

<Note>
LobeHub transforms agents from one-off tools into true teammates that understand your context, learn from your work, and collaborate with you across projects.
</Note>

## Why LobeHub?

Today's AI agents are task-driven tools that lack context, live in isolation, and require manual hand-offs. While some maintain memory, it's often global, shallow, and impersonal. Users are forced to toggle between fragmented conversations, making it difficult to form structured productivity.

**LobeHub changes everything.**

In LobeHub, we treat **agents as the unit of work**, providing an infrastructure where humans and agents co-evolve. Your agents maintain structured, editable memory, collaborate in groups, and adapt their behavior to act at the right moment.

## Core principles

### Agents as the unit of work

Building a personalized AI team starts with the Agent Builder. You can describe what you need once, and the agent setup starts right away, applying auto-configurations so you can use it instantly.

- **Unified intelligence**: Seamlessly access any model and any modality—all under your control
- **10,000+ skills**: Connect your agents to over 10,000 tools and MCP-compatible plugins
- **Personal memory**: Your agents build a clear understanding of your needs through continual learning

### Collaboration at scale

LobeHub introduces **Agent Groups**, allowing you to work with agents like real teammates. The system assembles the right agents for the task, enabling parallel collaboration and iterative improvement.

- **Pages**: Write and refine content with multiple agents in one place with shared context
- **Schedule**: Schedule runs and let agents do the work at the right time, even while you're away
- **Projects**: Organize work by project to keep everything structured and easy to track
- **Workspace**: Shared spaces for teams to collaborate with agents

### Co-evolution of humans and agents

The best AI is one that understands you deeply. LobeHub features **Personal Memory** that builds a clear understanding of your needs.

- **Continual learning**: Your agents learn from how you work, adapting their behavior
- **White-box memory**: Structured, editable memory gives you full control over what they remember
- **Transparency**: Watch AI reasoning unfold step-by-step with Chain of Thought visualization

## Open source and self-hosted

LobeHub is open source and can be self-hosted on your own infrastructure:

- Deploy with one click on Vercel, Zeabur, or Sealos
- Run with Docker for complete control
- Choose between local database (with CRDT sync) or PostgreSQL
- Full privacy protection with data stored locally
