LobeHub's Chain of Thought (CoT) visualization provides unprecedented transparency into how AI models think through complex problems, allowing you to observe the reasoning process in real-time.

## What is Chain of Thought?

Chain of Thought is a reasoning technique where AI models break down complex problems into clear, logical steps before providing a final answer. LobeHub makes this internal reasoning visible, so you can:

- **See the thinking process** - Watch how the AI approaches problems
- **Understand decision-making** - Follow the logical progression from question to answer
- **Validate reasoning** - Verify that conclusions are based on sound logic
- **Learn problem-solving** - Observe expert-level reasoning patterns
- **Debug responses** - Identify where reasoning might have gone wrong

## How It Works

When an AI model uses chain of thought reasoning:

1. **Problem Analysis** - The model first analyzes what's being asked
2. **Step-by-Step Thinking** - Breaks down the approach into logical steps
3. **Intermediate Reasoning** - Works through each step with visible thought process
4. **Final Answer** - Synthesizes insights into a clear response

All of this happens in real-time, with smooth animations showing the AI's thinking as it unfolds.

## Interpreting Chain of Thought

### Understanding the Steps

Each step in the reasoning chain typically shows:

- **Current focus** - What the AI is thinking about now
- **Reasoning** - Why this step matters
- **Conclusions** - What this step establishes
- **Next steps** - Where the logic is heading

### Reading the Flow

Look for:

**Logical progression:**
- Do steps build on each other?
- Are there clear connections between ideas?
- Does the reasoning flow naturally?

**Completeness:**
- Are all aspects addressed?
- Were edge cases considered?
- Is anything overlooked?

**Validity:**
- Are assumptions reasonable?
- Is the logic sound?
- Do conclusions follow from premises?

## Tips for Using Chain of Thought

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ask for explicit reasoning">
    Request that the AI "show its work" or "explain step-by-step" to encourage detailed chain of thought responses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review thinking for complex problems">
    Always expand and review the reasoning process for critical decisions or complex problems.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Learn from the patterns">
    Study how the AI approaches different types of problems to improve your own problem-solving skills.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Question unexpected reasoning">
    If a reasoning step seems off, ask for clarification or alternative approaches.
  </Accordion>

  </Accordion>
